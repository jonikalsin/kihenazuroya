Who is Ricky Gervais wife Jane Fallon?

Ricky Gervais is an English comedian best known for his work on the television series The Office. He was born on June 25, 1961, in Reading, Berkshire, England (2001–03).

After finishing his philosophy studies at the University of London, Gervais headed the little-known band Seona Dancing, which had a little hit in the Philippines with the song “More to Lose” in 1985.

In 1996, he joined a new radio station, XFM, and aired a show there, where he met Stephen Merchant, who became a regular collaborator. During the 1990s, Gervais wrote and performed comedic routines for the BBC TV programs Bruiser and Golden Years. He began as a regular on The 11 O’Clock Show and briefly hosted his own talk show, Meet Ricky Gervais, in 2000.

The Office, a satire of the modern office, premiered on BBC television in July 2001. The situation comedy, created and written by Gervais and Merchant, uses a faux documentary approach to follow the ups and downs of the workforce of Wernham Hogg, a fictional paper firm.

Who is Ricky Gervais wife Jane Fallon?

Jane Elizabeth Mary Fallon (born 9 December 1960) is a television producer and author from England.
Fallon has been married to comedian Ricky Gervais since 1982 when they met at University College London. The pair have been married since 1984 and live in Hampstead. Elsie Fallon, Fallon’s niece, is an actress.
