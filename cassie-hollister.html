Cassie Hollister is an American YouTuber who is best known for uploading pranks, challenges, reactions, comedy videos, and vlogs on her channel ‘SoCassie‘. Cassie Hollister’s early social-media fame came from her popularity on Instagram.

Cassie Hollister first opened her eyes on June 26, 1997, in California, USA. She is currently 26 years old and her sun sign is Cancer.

She was born to Jim and Suzanne Hollister. Moreover, she has two siblings named Missy and Jimmy. She has nephews named Oliver and Finley.

Furthermore, she thought of taking up business studies in college. At one point, She learned kickboxing and Jiu-jitsu. Her husband, Carlos, is trained in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Career and Professional Life

Cassie first kicked off social media through her Instagram account. She posts her selfies and other random family pictures.

In addition to being popular on Instagram, she is also popular on Twitter with over 60,000 followers. She first joined Twitter in September 2012 but didn’t post her first tweet until a year later.

She always wanted to own a YouTube channel and create vlogs. However, she waited until she graduated. After her graduation, she thought it was the right time to start her YouTube career.

She joined the YouTube community on February 12, 2016, with her channel ‘SoCassie‘. The channel primarily hosts viral challenges, pranks, storytimes, DIY projects, and outdoor activities.

She has also done review videos for the channel. She lets her fans know her better with her Q&A videos. It is a family-oriented channel, and she features her family members in most of her vlogs and videos.

She began her debut ‘YouTube venture with a video named ‘5 Facts About Me,’ published on March 1, 2016. The first video on the channel to reach a million views was ‘Short Girl Problems! – SoCass’.
