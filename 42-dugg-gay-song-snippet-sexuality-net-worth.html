Is 42 Dugg Gay? Leaked Song Lyrics Snippet Sparks His Sexuality Debates

42 Dugg became the top-notch story on 21 July 2020 on Twitter after his new track was leaked. The leaked song has aroused the rumors of the Detroit rapper's sexuality because numerous tweets cited that the lyrics were misheard from the snippets.

The leaked track is untitled yet, where 42 Dugg raps in a slurred and mumbled flow; however, listeners thought that he sang the song about oral sex (oral stimulation of a man's penis) to get money to pay his rent. The lyrics state: 'I was out here sucking d**k I was tryin pay the rent.'

Concerning the same line, several memes and gifs are prevailing all over the social media platforms.

Fans were quick to respond to the questionable track after its release. One of the fans responded, '42 dugg was doing whaaa???' and another one followed with a question, '42 Dugg turned into a Glizzy Goblin to keep a roof over his head?'

The lyrics of the song were enough to create the speculations about whether 42 Dugg was gay or something.

But the rising Detroit rapper immediately approached his fans and addressed the original lyrics. As per him, the real line which came out to be a controversial one was 'HOES OUT HERE SUCKIN D**K… I WAS TRYNA PAY HER RENT.'

However, the fans were not silenced even after getting the real lyrics. One said, 'Nah s**t was loud and clear' while another stated 'I'm saying tho I don't hear 'hoe' at all. There were some understanding listeners as well, out of which one responded, 'You just gotta enunciate your words more, buddy.' Similarly, another wrote, 'Okay we just wanted to know we thought u was tripping.'

Who Is 42 Dugg? His Net Worth

42 Dugg is an American rap artist from Detroit, Michigan. He is widely acclaimed for his songs like We Paid, World On My Shoulder, Not A Rapper, Bounce Back, Dog Food, It Get Deeper, Tripping, and many others. In the songs like Grace and We Paid, he collaborated with another artist Lil Baby. As a rapper, he is jointly signed by Lil Baby and Yo Gotti through Empire Records.

Till the date, 42 Dugg has brought three studio albums, including 11241 Wayburn, Young and Turnt, and Young & Turnt, Vol. 2. Also, he has one Extended Plays (EPs), namely 11241 Wayburn, Pt. 2 in his account.

From his music career, 42 Dugg has an estimated net worth of about $500 Thousand.
