The Real Story Behind Emily Ratajkowskis Parents

Emily’s mother, Kathleen, is a teacher, while her father, John David, hails from Coronado, California, and is an accomplished artist.

John Ratajkowski is skilled in sculpture, painting, and printmaking, with his work showcased in more than 25 galleries and exhibitions across the United States and Europe.

In a father-daughter interview for Cultured, Emily’s father expressed, “I mean, your mother is a writer, and I’m a visual artist. So it was an obvious step to me when you moved into acting.

I knew whatever you did was going to be art-related. It’s just who we are.”

He continued, “My feeling about life, in general, is to make things first and talk about them later, you know? Raising you, I wanted to put everything out there.

Ideas, materials, questions, and leave you alone with it.”

John added, “Afterward, I’d tell you what I thought was good and what you could do better.

You always found your way through your art and your career.”
