Trevor Ariza Bio (Wiki)

Who is Trevor Ariza?

Tall and handsome Trevor Ariza is a well-known American professional basketball player. He is best known as the player for the Houston Rockets of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He often plays as a shooting guard and small forward position wearing a jersey number 1.

Trevor Ariza: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

American professional basketball player Trevor Ariza was born in Miami, Florida. He was born on 30 June 1985. He is of African American ethnicity and has American nationality. Currently, he is 36 years old.

His birth name is Trevor Anthony Ariza. He is the son of Lolita Ariza(mother) and Trevor Saunders(father). He has two brothers Tajh Ariza and Kenny Ariza.

Since childhood, he had built up a strong interest in basketball.

In which school did Trevor Ariza attend?

He studied at Westchester High School in Los Angeles where he was considered a five-star recruit by Rivals.com. For his further study, he attended UCLA.

Trevor Ariza: Professional Life and Career

Trevor Ariza has been playing basketball since 2004.  He started playing basketball professionally after being selected by New York Knicks in the 2004 NBA draft. He became the youngest player to ever wear and play for the Knicks.

He was traded to Orlando Magic along with Penny Hardaway in exchange for Steve Francis in February 2006. He was soon traded to Los Angeles Lakers in November 2007.

He missed several matches due to a bone injury in his right foot. He recorded a career-high of 21 points against a match with Utah Jazz.  He has been a team member of the Lakers winning a 2009 NBA Championship.

On 3 July 2009, he signed with Houston Rockets for over five years worth $33 million but he was soon traded to New Orleans Hornets on 11 August 2010.

On 20 June 2012, Ariza was traded to Washington Wizards along with Emeka Okafor in exchange for Rashard Lewis.

While playing for the Wizards, he recorded a career-high of 40 points including eight 3-points against a match with the Philadelphia 76ers. On 15 July 2014, he was acquired by the Rockets and since then he has been playing for the team.
