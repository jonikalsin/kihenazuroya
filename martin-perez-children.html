Martn Pérez Jiménez, a Venezuelan professional baseball pitcher for the Texas Rangers of Major League Baseball, was born on April 4, 1991.

He formerly played for the Minnesota Twins and Boston Red Sox in Major League Baseball.

He throws and bats left-handed and is listed as 6 feet 0 inches (1.83 m) and 200 pounds (91 kg).

Pérez signed a one-year contract with the Boston Red Sox on December 19, 2019, which includes a team option for the 2021 season.

Pérez pitched in 12 games (all starts) for the 2020 Red Sox, posting a 3–5 record with a 4.50 ERA and 46 strikeouts in 62 innings pitched.

He resigned with the Red Sox that same year in a one year deal.

Pérez signed a one-year, $4 million contract with the Texas Rangers on March 14, 2022.
