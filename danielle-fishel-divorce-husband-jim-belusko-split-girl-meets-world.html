Danielle Fishel Divorce Calls It Quits With Husband After 3 Years Hollywood Life

This is heartbreaking! Danielle and Tim called it quits back in 2015, People reports. The couple managed to keep their divorce quiet for months, even after it was finalized back in March. The duo was married back in 2013 at a “short and sweet” wedding in Los Angeles that allegedly had her friend and Boy Meets World co-star Ben Savage in attendance.

Danielle met Tim after her Boy Meets World days, when she went to Cal State, Fullerton to get her degree. While she was there, she was a math tutor and met Tim, a fellow student. The couple dated for more than three years before tying the knot, and were married for another three before calling it off.

Danielle and Tim have no children together, and Danielle seems fine since the split. She’s continued starring on Girl Meets World, the Disney revamp of her original TV show, and she seems to really love her young co-stars, who she frequently posts selfies with on Instagram. We hope she finds love soon and can move on and be happy!
