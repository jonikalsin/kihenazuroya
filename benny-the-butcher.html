Who is Benny The Butcher:

Benny The Butcher is a famous Rapper. He was born on November 27, 1985 and his birthplace is United States. Benny is also well known as, Underground rapper who’s risen to fame as 1/3 of the Griselda Records rap crew along with his cousins Alvin Worthy a.k.a. Westside Gunn and Conway the Machine. He’s released five albums and collaborated on several mix tapes.

Benny is originated from United States. He collaborated with Mayhem Lauren on “Echo Long” from Tana Talk 3.
