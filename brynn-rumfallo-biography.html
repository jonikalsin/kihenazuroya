Brynn Rumfallo Bio (Wiki)

Who is Brynn Rumfallo?

Brynn Rumfallo is a dancer and a model. She is one of the best dancers in the industry and has got the maximum number of dancing skills.

She is also known for Club Dance in Arizona.

Brynn Rumfallo: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

Brynn Rumfallo was born on 1 July 2003 in Arizona, USA. She was the daughter of Aaron and Ashlee. Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is Caucasian.

Her father’s name is Aaron Rumfallo and her mother’s name is Ashlee Terry Rumfallo.

She was the eldest one among her siblings Noah and another named Sadie. She started to dance when she was 2 years old.

Brynn Rumfallo loved to dance a lot as dance was a part of her life. She also performed in many kinds of programs in her school days.

Brynn Rumfallo was one of the dancers of the group named Fresh Faces.

Education History

About her education history, Brynn Rumfallo attends Highland high school and she is studying in the ninth standard.

Brynn Rumfallo: Professional Life, Career

Brynn Rumfallo gave a start in her career as a dancer. She took part in America’s Got Talent as a dancer. She represented herself as a member of a group named Fresh Faces.

With her team, she went to the quarterfinals. She also joined another show named Dance Moms. She also danced with Josh Groban on stage.

Brynn wanted to be a backup dancer for Katy Perry. She also got the opportunity to dance in the Ellen Degeneres Show. She also got the Best Mini Dancer at the 2014 Dance Awards.

As she was a brand ambassador of Miss Fit by Miss Behave, she was invited as a guest in the Miss Fit booth at DancerPalooza 2016.

Brynn Rumfallo also performed her solo as Forever in DancerPalooza. She was also featured in a dance video of Todrick Hall and MattyB.

She has also been a model for Miss Behave Girls, the California Kisses, and the Purple Pixies.
