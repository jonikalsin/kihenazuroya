Biography

Sebastian Danzig was born in Toronto, Canada on July 1, 1992. Best known as the guitarist for the Canadian art-rock band Palaye Royale, he and lead singer Remington Leith were named champions of MTV’s Musical March Madness in 2014. Their single “Get Higher” was also chosen for an internationally distributed Samsung Galaxy Note commercial. He and Palaye Royale beat out Chester Bennington and the rest of Linkin Park in the finale round of Musical March Madness 2014. Palaye Royale formed in late 2011. By 2012, they had released their debut single “Morning Light.”
