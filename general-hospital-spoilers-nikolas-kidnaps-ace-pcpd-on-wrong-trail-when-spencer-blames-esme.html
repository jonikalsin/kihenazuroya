General Hospital spoilers and rumors tease Nikolas Cassadine (Adam Huss) might kidnap Ace Prince-Cassadine (Joey and Jay Clay) but Spencer Cassadine (Nicholas Alexander Chavez) will blame Esme Prince (Avery Kristen Pohl).

General Hospital Spoilers: Laura Collins Reports The Mother And Son Missing

It will be Laura Collins (Genie Francis) who actually makes the missing persons report on Esme and Ace, after Alexis Davis (Nancy Lee Grahn) reports she didn’t show up for work.

GH Spoilers: Laura Collins Must Report The Bad News To Spencer Cassadine

Esme is Kevin Collins’ (Jon Lindstrom) niece and Ace is Spencer’s brother so she has to tell Kevin and Spencer; Spencer suspects Esme’s memory and the old Esme are back.

General Hospital Spoilers: Dante Falconeri And Harrison Chase Arrive

Family members of missing persons usually start the investigation so their fingerprints are expected to be there-Spencer interrupts, in spite of Laura’s telling him to be quiet.

But even at that, Dante and Chase approach the missing persons investigation as exactly that and no one thinks to look for evidence of Nikolas’ kidnapping Esme and Ace.
